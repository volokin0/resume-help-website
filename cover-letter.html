Cover Letter Tips

1. Start Strong

Begin your cover letter by addressing the hiring manager directly and stating your enthusiasm for the position.

2. Focus on Skills

Highlight specific skills you have that align with the job description. Show how your background can contribute to the company.

3. Show Enthusiasm

Express your genuine interest in the company and the role, demonstrating you’ve done your research and want to be part of their team.

4. Keep it Concise

Avoid lengthy explanations. Keep your cover letter short and to the point—no more than one page.
