Tips for Writing a Resume

1. Start Strong with a Summary

Begin your resume with a strong, concise summary that highlights your top skills and experiences.

2. Focus on Achievements, Not Responsibilities

Use action verbs and quantify your achievements (e.g., “Increased sales by 20% in 6 months”).

3. Tailor Your Resume to the Job

Customize your resume for each job application to show how your skills fit the role.
